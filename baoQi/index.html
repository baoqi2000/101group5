Video Games

Do you like video games?

Video games have become one of the most popular entertainment and profitable industries in China in the past two decades. China has the world's largest number of esports players and has become the largest esports consumer market in the world. According to research, there are 552 million Chinese video game players in 2022(Lai Lin Thomala, 2023). Also, the revenue of Chinese esports continues to increase from 2014 to 2019. Chinese esports is rapidly developing with a huge compound growth rate of 132.2% and reaching RMB 102.8 billion in revenues by 2019(Yang Yue et al., 2020). This industry develops rapidly and promotes within a short time so it is valuable for people to analyze. I argue that support from the Chinese government, technological innovation, and the appearance of live streaming are three primary factors that account for the rapid development of the video game industry in China in recent years. Supportive policies from the Chinese government encourage more people to engage in this field and build video game organizations or clubs. Also, the development of new technology contributes to a new genre of video games named mobile gaming. It attracts a lot of new players and is more convenient than traditional console games. Besides, live streaming creates job opportunities for players and increases the popularity of many video games. Finally, I will discuss and mention other essential elements such as the usage of social media and the influence of globalization, which boost the rapid development of the Chinese video game industry as well. In the past two decades, the video game industry in China developed so fast from a small-scale industry to a giant field. It is worth analyzing how this industry changes and its development strategies. Many high-tech companies, investment companies, and companies from different fields look forward to finding out its development secrets.
